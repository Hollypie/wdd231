About Me

Hello! I'm Holly, a student at BYU-Idaho pursuing a bachelor's degree in software development through online classes. Over the years, I've developed a passion for board gaming, which started as a fun way to spend time with my kids. What began as a casual hobby has turned into an exciting journey of discovering new games while always returning to old favorites. This website is a little corner of the web where I share my love for board games. I hope it sparks some joy and helps others find new games to enjoy with family and friends, just like I have. Thanks for visiting, and I hope you enjoy exploring my page!
